How does the 15-day free trial work?

After you create an account, you get instant access to GleeShift for 15 days. You will not be charged anything unless you decide to keep your account open.

What happens at the end of the trial?

We will email you before your trial expires. You can choose if you would like to keep your account open. Your trial account will become your permanent account (all of your customizations, data, and actions remain intact).

Do you offer non-profit or franchise discounts?

Yes, we have discounts for non-profits or educational organizations. please contact us.

How is the total number of employees calculated?

When an employee is added into the account they are included in the total employees, if you delete an employee they will not be included in the count unless you restore them.

Are there any set up or cancellation fees?

No. You can cancel your subscription at any time. There is no set up fee or cancellation fee.

What if we are a seasonal company?

In this case, you can simply pause your subscription for the months you don’t need. Once you need GleeShift again you can reactivate your subscription and all of your data will be exactly how you left it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept check, bank transfer and all major credit cards, including VISA, MasterCard and American Express.

What if we want to cancel?

Renewal can be cancelled at any time before the expiration date to stop the next renewal, No refunds are given for unused portions of paid subscriptions
